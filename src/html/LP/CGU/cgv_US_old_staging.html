However, purchasing an e-gift card does not earn KusmiKlub points.

Limited Liability

Orientis accepts no liability for e-gift cards that are expired, lost, stolen, damaged, errored, or used fraudulently.

Returns Involving Goods Purchased on the Site Using an e-Gift Card

A right of withdrawal can be exercised under the terms set forth in Article 6 only if the e-gift card was not used before the end of the indicated period.
If a refund is being requested for Products purchased with an e-gift card, the amount of the Products corresponding to the amount of the e-gift card will be refunded by means of a credit in the form of a new unique generated code valid for one (1) year from its date of issue.
